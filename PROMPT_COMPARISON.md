## Expected Visual Improvement

### Problem with v2 prompts:
The AI was told "TWO-PAGE SPREAD" and "GUTTER", so it logically drew a vertical line down the middle to represent where the pages would meet at the book's spine.

### Solution with v3 prompts:
The AI is now told "WIDE PANORAMIC" with "seamless continuous composition - no dividing lines", so it will treat it as a single unified image with natural flow from left to right through the center.

**The center zone is now explicitly described as:**
- Forest spread: "creek, trees, dappled light" (nature elements, not a dividing line)
- Sunset spread: "ocean, sky, sunset glow" (atmospheric elements, not a dividing line)  
- Space spread: "cosmic darkness, stars, nebulae" (space elements, not a dividing line)
